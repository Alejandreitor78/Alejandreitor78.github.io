ABOUT

The Land of Doom is an adventurous and mysterious place! Full of dangerous and poisonous creatures, it is suitable for people of all ages! It was founded by Mr. X in 1905, and is still (but barely) in business today! To learn more about this incredible place, go to our travel website!

TICKETS

To get into The Land of Doom, you must have a ticket. To buy tickets go to the register in the lobby when you arrive. The price of tickets vary depending on which day it is. On Monday through Friday tickets are $1,000 a-piece, and on Saturdays they're $1,500 a-piece. If it's a holiday they're $2,000 a-piece. So if it's Saturday and it's a holiday, then you'll have to pay the grand total of $2,500 dollars. But if any fatalities happen during your trip, you will get the ride home half off!

HOTELS

Need a place to stay for the night? You're at the right place! The Land of Doom offers a variety of places to spend the night! Here are some of the places you can sleep:

  1. Below the Volcano of Death!
  2. In the vast and dry desert!
  3. On top of The Tallest Mountain!
  4. Under The Ocean of Death!
